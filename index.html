Kansas City

Kansas City is home to me. It is the right size city where there is a lot of stuff to do, but it's not so busy that there are people everywhere. It has some of the best barbeque on the planet and you get the nice midwest hospital.

Directions to Kansas City from Los Angeles

  1. Get on US-101 S from N Main St
  2. Follow I-15 N and I-70 E to Page St in KCMO. Take exit 2E from I-70 E
    1. You are on this road for 1616 miles
  3. Take Oak St to your destination
